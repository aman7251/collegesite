Central Library

The technological advancements have transformed libraries from a mere static storehouse of documents to a dynamic powerhouse of information, serving the stakeholders in utilizing useful and need-based collection of knowledge resources. To sustain in the knowledge society, the libraries should switch over from the traditional system to digital to provide quick information service and to save the precious time of the users. Over the past few decades, with the changes in library collections, services and demands of users, and also with tremendous impact of the IT based technologies, many innovative services are being introduced by libraries to cope up with the changes.

The Ultra Mega 1.8K Hostel

The Institute has excellent residential facilities for all the students admitted to the various courses. There are twenty hostel blocks (halls of residence) for men and two for women. Hostel administration is managed by a Chief Warden and a team of Wardens drawn from the faculty and appointed by the Director on a tenure basis. Hostel Advisory Council headed by the Director is responsible for major policy decisions with respect to the hostel administration. Hostel Executive Committee takes decisions on routine matters. Students have representation on both the administrative bodies. Hostel office has full-fledged supporting staff and other workers.
